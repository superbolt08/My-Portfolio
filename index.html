My story

My name is Syed Saad Ali. A UBC computer science student dedicated towards taking my degree towards sustainable development. I initially pursued computer science with a passion for game development. However, I soon discovered the vast opportunities for interdisciplinary collaboration that technology offers. With this realization, I decided to channel my skills towards sustainable development, aiming to make a meaningful impact through innovation.

Why Sustainability?

A big desire I have from my career is to make it meaningful, to have a positive impact. Upon research, I discovered the numerous opportunities that computer science offers in sustainable development, such as creating energy-saving algorithms, using AI to predict natural disasters, and harnessing IoT and data science for environmental monitoring. These possibilities are so exciting that I find myself wanting to explore them all. Moreover, sustainable development is crucial in today's world, making this work not only fascinating but also essential for a better future.
